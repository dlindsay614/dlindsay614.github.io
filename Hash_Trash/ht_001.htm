H3SC Trash No. 1
Full Moon
August 12, 2003

What an absolutely AWESOME evening. 

The pack was out around 7:30 PM from the riverside of Ocean Ave. in Mel Beach.  We meandered through the neighborhoods with all those nice big expensive houses (our visitors from the UK were impressed), through the school sportsgrounds, then some shiggy and across the road to the beach.  A relatively short trail enjoyed by all, but the best was yet to come. 

The Full Moon had not yet arrived so we circled up and had some fun.  A few Kojak stories to get everyone in a playful mood, especially the blondies, and then the usual down downs for sinners, visitors, FRBs, 50% rule etc.  And then we swung low while watching the beautiful display of phosphorescent waves in a vivid blue color breaking on the beach.  WOW.

Then Hoosier Daddy broke out the pulled pork , buns and fixins to kinda top off our liquid bellies, mmmmmm good. 

But, wait, the best is yet to come.  Finally, old Full Moon started to make its appearance by painting the cloud stack behind which it was hiding a nice vivid pink.  And then it appeared in all its majesty.  And, the weather was pleasantly cool to beat.  This is Florida in August?  Even long time Floridians were thoroughly impressed.  How can you beat being on a gorgeous deserted beach, very few bugs, comfortably cool, the full moon bright in the sky, Mars a bit below at about 8:00 o'clock, and those AWESOME blue waves breaking in the surf? 

And a great bunch of Hashers to boot.  Doesn't get any better than this.

In fact, when some of us (well, me anyway) decided it was time for bed we couldn't get anyone to leave.  Some of the Harriettes even stayed with some of the left-over beer while the rest got a ride to our vehicles.  Maybe I should drive down there, they may still be there.

Thank you Hoosier, Mullet and everyone else for an absolutely PERFECT evening.

And for those who missed it, you missed it.

ON ONnn

Kojak
