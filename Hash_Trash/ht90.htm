HASH TRASH

H3SC Run #90

Hare: Harry Cheezballz

Hounds: Kojak, Lollipop, Timmy, Shop Teacher, Snippit, Just Mary,
Just Angel

Shiggy factor was approximately 80% (razor sharp palmettos, a little
water, burnt forest area, pine forest area sand and a little pavement)

FRB: Snippit
DFL: Kojak

It was a short trail, approximately 1.9 miles. Started out with a
little pavement from the Running Zone to the gas station on Wickham.
Shop Teacher in the lead to find the CB3. What do you expect from a
dyslexic haring engineer. Trail was found heading into the burnt out
palmetto fields on the Wickhams west side. Out of the shaggy onto
Parkway, Shop Teacher ripped a 2X4 post out of the ground for a
bridge to cross the small ditch. Check mark across the road,Shop and
Snippit go one way, Just Angel heads west (wrong). Shop splits into
the shiggy, Snippit stays east bound on the road,on true trail. They
meet back at the safe crossing before Wickham.Across Wickham into a
short shiggy portion on the south side of Parkway. Then came the
check that takes the hounds across Parkway to the first check inside
Wickham Park.Trail loops through the west forest of the park then
onto the road into a check, Shop & Snippit could not find trail at
the check. They ended up ranging on the road. Found trail down a
ways and missed a little shiggy. Meanwhile behind them Just Mary and
Angel found true trail where the others lost trail. Took the wrong
turn to catch the CB5. Lollipop,Kojak and Timmy were able to follow
true trail thanks to the Just Angel and Just Mary. Finished behind
the flooring shop on the west side Wickham.

Good time at circle, on after was at Dong Loggers on Post.
